Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DevConf 2026.

Who can attend the conference?

Anyone interested in technology, software development, design, startups, or innovation is welcome to attend.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. All registered participants will receive an official certificate of participation after the event.

Are meals included with the ticket?

Yes. Lunch, refreshments, and coffee breaks are included with every ticket.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Refund requests are accepted up to 7 days before the event. After that, tickets become non-refundable.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes. Free parking is available for all conference attendees.
